ABOUT SNIST

Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology

Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST), established in 1997 in Hyderabad, is one of the premier engineering institutions known for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development. The institution has built a strong reputation for providing quality technical education with a perfect balance of academics, research, practical learning, and industry exposure.

SNIST offers a modern and vibrant campus environment with advanced laboratories, smart classrooms, Wi-Fi enabled infrastructure, central library, auditoriums, sports facilities, premium hostels, cafeterias, and innovation centers. The college encourages students to actively participate in technical events, research activities, cultural programs, leadership opportunities, and student clubs to enhance their overall personality and professional skills.

With highly experienced faculty, industry-oriented curriculum, strong placement support, and collaborations with leading organizations, SNIST helps students become skilled, confident, and future-ready professionals. The institution continuously focuses on creativity, innovation, teamwork, ethics, and lifelong learning to prepare students for successful careers and leadership roles in the modern world.
